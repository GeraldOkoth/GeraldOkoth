About Gerald


Date of birth


Gerald Okoth Otieno was born to Grace Aoko Ochoo and George Otieno Awuor, on 6 September, 2003 at Kisumu district hospital, in Kisumu city.

Gerald has been raised since his childhood in the village kanyakwar in Kisumu central constituency.


Gerald is the forth child of the five siblings, in a family of seven.



Education background


Gerald began his schooling at St. Paul's Kanyakwar Primary School where he say his KCPE in 2015 and excelled. He attained 318 marks out of the possible 500 marks.

He then proceeded to St. Anthony Dago Kokore for his high school education.
He sat his KCSE in November 2019. His hardwork, commitment and dedication to his studies rewarded him greatly by achieving a mean grade of Be (plain) with 62 points out of the possible 84 points.

Gerald developed interest interest and enthusiasm in computing and therefore made a resolution to pursue a degree in mathematics and computer science in higher learning institution

Gerald was invited to join Machakos University in Machakos Kenya where he is currently pursuing his undergraduate degree in the preferred course.



Religious background


Gerald was born and raised in a Catholic family where he has cherished his catholic faith and hold it to the moment.
Gerald is a staunch Christian who has offered to serve God with passion.
Gerald is a baptized and confirmed catholic and receives the holy eucharist during mass.

He is a member of the choir both at home and in campus where he served as the choir chairman for a couple of years, since he joined campus in 2020.


In addition, Gerald expressed his interest in music ~playing piano. He therefore decided to join music classes in campus where he acquired both theoretical and practical skills which has seen him teach music and play piano for the catholic university choir.



Hobbies


Gerald is a professional pianist, consequently he plays the piano during church service in campus in collaboration with other young talented students.
He has also mentored other young stude who have developed interest in music. This he does with a lot of passion since he enjoys music.

Gerald plays chess and has participated in chess games with his friends in campus. He began playing chess while in high school where he received mentorship form his former highschool principal.

Gerald is a football fan and weigh his support behind Chelsea football club, an English premier league football club.
Internationally, he supports France which won the wor cup in 2018 and also the runners up in the 2022 FIFA world cup.
