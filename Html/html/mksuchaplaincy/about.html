Machakos University CSA is an association of all catholic
students within Machakos University.
The chaplaincy consists of vibrant young students with
a strong background in faith.
The CSA embraces fully the catholic faith that is practiced
worldwide.

The association is led by a chaplain and matron who
are the head of the chaplaincy. Besides the chaplain and
the matron there exist a leadership structure that ensures
the smooth operation of the chaplaincy activities, this
include but not limited to the following:

  1. The Executive
  2. The Committee
