Machakos University Catholic Students Association

There are several groups within the chaplaincy. They include the following:

  1. Jumuias
  2. Vocation
  3. Liturgical dancers
  4. Choir
  5. Legion of Mary
  6. Precious ladies
  7. Pioneer/Gents
  8. Welfare

  1. The Executive

    2. The Executive is the main body that govern the entire CSA activities and its operation.

    The executive consist of seven elected members. They inlude the following:

    • Chairman
    • Chairlady
    • Organising Secretary
    • Vice Organising secretary
    • Secretary
    • Vice Secretary
    • Liturgist

    1. Chairman

      2. The chairman is the overall head of the leadership structure

      Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Co-ordination of all the group's activities.
      2. Calling and cheering meeting of the group.
      3. Advocating for unity and active participation of the group members.
      4. Settling disputes in the group if any.
      5. He/she is among the signatories of the group

    2. Chairlady

      3. She is the principle assistant of the chairman, and undertakes the following roles:

      1. Co-ordination of all the group's activities.
      2. Calling and cheering meeting of the group.
      3. Advocating for unity and active participation of the group members.
      4. Settling disputes in the group if any.
      5. He/she is among the signatories of the group

    3. Organising Secretary

      4. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Makes daily announcements.
      2. Posts the CSA activities and programs on the notice boards
      3. Acts as the chair in absence of the chairpersons.
      4. Responsible for uniting and bringing peace among the committee.

    4. Vice Organising Secretary

      5. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Makes daily announcements.
      2. Posts the CSA activities and programs on the notice boards
      3. Acts as the chair in absence of the chairpersons.
      4. Responsible for uniting and bringing peace among the committee.

    5. Secretary

      6. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Writes and reads minutes of the groups meetings.
      2. Write letter to other groups with the aid of the chairperson.
      3. Keep records of the group.
      4. Acts as the treasurer when the treasurer is away.

    6. Vice Secretary

      7. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Carry-out the roles of the secretary in case absent.

    7. Treasurer

      8. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. Keep all the records of the group's expenditure.
      2. Read or updates the commit of the financial report.
      3. Keeps money collected from members.

    8. Liturgist

      9. Duties and Responsibilities
      1. In charge of Alter by ensuring everything is in place before mass in co-ordination with the librarians
      2. Ensure the priest is assisted during mass delivery i.e The priest is not supposed to move microphone by him self, he should be assisted.
      3. Organise litugical dancing.

  2. The committee

The committee works hand in hand with the executive to ensure smooth operations of the chaplaincy activities. They do this by organising events and activities of the church. The following are members of the committee:.

  • Chairperons
  • Choir Treasurer
  • Welfare
